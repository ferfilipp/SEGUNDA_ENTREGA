    • Academy

    "Studying letter design opened a fascinating world to me. Once I found it, I just didn't want to leave anymore; and this is one of the reasons why I teach: I want everyone to discover this world and I'm confident I can show them the way. I believe that drawing letterforms is something anyone can learn and that the best way to achieve success is by first learning the foundations and then developing an effective process that lets you confidently create great work. Over the last 10 years, I have studied Communication Design in Escola Elisava, mastered Type & Media​ in the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague, worked with numerous clients, and taught thousands of students all over the world. Inside my courses I want to share with you the knowledge I have gathered over these last 10 years, so that you too can become a master at hand lettering, thrive in the creative industries and create unique designs that stand out in the crowd. You should be always learning from those who are already where you want to be and doing what you want to do; but... it’s not just about copying their models or templates. Instead it’s about understanding the rules of the game of letter design so that you can turn it into your creative playground and reach your full potential as an artist. I have done it, my students have done it, and I’m confident you can do it too. So join us here at the academy and let’s help you develop your skills and flourish in your freelance business."
